## 💫 About Me
I’m **Kalavakuri Abhinay**, a **Data Scientist from IIT Bhilai** with hands-on experience as both a **Data Science Intern** and a **Data Analyst Intern**.

I specialize in building **end-to-end ML systems** ; from raw data, feature engineering, and modeling to **robust evaluation, business-aligned decision thresholds, and deployment**.

My work spans **large-scale tabular ML**, **NLP & RAG systems**, **computer vision**, and **data analytics for business decision-making**, with a strong focus on **model reliability, explainability, and real-world impact**.
